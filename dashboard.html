Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Apollo Verifier handles your data.

1. Data We Collect

  • Lead data — names, job titles, company names, email addresses, LinkedIn URLs, and company websites scraped from pages you visit.
  • API Keys — your Apify API token(s), stored locally in chrome.storage.local.
  • Verification results — email validation outcomes returned by the Apify email-checker actor.

2. How We Store Data

  • Locally: All data is stored in chrome.storage.local on your device.
  • Cloud (Turso): If you use the cloud sync feature, profile data is stored in a Turso SQLite database associated with your account. No data is shared with third parties.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect browsing history, passwords, payment information, or any data beyond what is explicitly listed above.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Apify — used for email verification. Emails are sent to Apify's API for validation. Review Apify's Privacy Policy.
  • Turso — used for cloud storage of lead data. Review Turso's Privacy Policy.

5. Your Rights

You can delete all your data at any time using the "Clear All" button in the dashboard. This removes data from both local storage and the Turso cloud database.

6. Contact

For privacy-related enquiries, contact the developer via the Chrome Web Store listing.
